Koode

The Koode project was formed on the theme, building a sanctuary for women, by women, to create a safe playground for children, shelter for vulnerable women & children, elderly and differently-abled people of Perinthalmanna town and neighbouring communities on the vision of a society free from the trauma of abuse, crime, devastation, desolation, disability, fear, ill-health, ignorance, illiteracy & isolation.

To end crime against women and children, Koodai, a registered charity came into existence on 9th February 2019 with a heart warming and powerful inaugural programme including dance, music and drama, telling the stories about women in crisis, reaching across the barriers of castes and religions, politics and poverty, health and education, Koodai offers friendship and companionship, safe space for talents and creativity, skills development and support, educational and health support, legal and financial help, to provide hope, encouragement and sanctuary to women and the most vulnerable in our society.

Koode, a community of women enthusiasts, committed in serving in the field of philanthropic services to the victims of Domestic violence, Sexually Abused Children and women, differently-abled, Mentally Challenged persons, Widows and Aged people. We provide financial help for hapless girls for education , palliative assistance to the helpless patients , adopting families living in deplorable conditions.

Members of 'Koode' are socially committed women, consisting of lawyers, doctors, teachers, civil servants professionally qualified social workers, counsellors, home makers involved in supporting our fellow beings who are shattered and traumatized due to different social issues, and unstable economy.

Koode is a registered charitable society and our office address is Koode, Near Kakkooth GMLP School, Valiyangadi, Ooty Road, Perintalmanna, Malappuram District and our email id is koodeperintalmanna@gmail.com.

Our President is Dr. Febeena seethi, Consultant physiatrist (MD, PMR physical medicine and rehabilitation). She is also Executive member Pain and palliative, Past secretary Women doctors wing Perintalmanna, Managing director of Terapia ladies fitness centre (a wellness clinic for mind and body wellbeing of women).

Our Secretary is Adv. Indira Nair, She is Taluk Legal Service Authority Member, Court Mediator, Distric Kaval Plus victim Advocate, Jagratha samithi Advocate in 5 Panchayaths, Melattur Mahila Samacham Executive Member, Saketham old age home member, Balasadanam Orphanage member. We have a total of 21 Executive Members and 260 Members overall.

MISSION AND VISION OF THE SOCIETY

Mission
Reaching across the barriers of caste, religion and politics, koode offers friendship and companionship, safe space for talents and creativity, skill development and support, educational and health support, legal and financial help,to provide hope encouragement and sanctuary to women and the most vulnernerable in the society.

Vision
A society free from the trauma of abuse, crime, devastation, desolation, disability, fear, ill-health, ignorance, illeteracy and isolation.

FUNCTIONAL AREAS

1. Counselling and psychological support
Busy lives, shaky family relationships, challenging finances, uncertain days... Men, women, children and the elderly alike go through many stresses. Together, Koode realized that the upliftment of the society means moulding individuals to face life with courage.

Our counselling wing is led by three clinical psychologists Dr. Smitha Rahman, Ms. Rasula Sharafudheen and Ms Raseena Mehroof. Ms. Sabitha jayakrishnan deals with children having leaning disabilities. We have five volunteers who have undergone training under M hat (mental health action trust). In our office we give regular counselling support to the needy women free of cost.

2. Support for girl's Education
Koode has been doing phenomenal works in promoting children's education contributing to numerous children's higher studies and at the younger stages too, who sought refuge with them fearing that their education would be stopped midway, have been supported to provide fees, laptops and smart phones.

3.Day care centre for elderly persons
Koode is Conducting Day care centre with Perintalmanna palliative care Society. Every Saturday from 9.00 am to 4 pm for the elderly people who are confined within the four walls of the house. It has been decided to prepare a day care, to listen, entertain and serve nutritious food for them.

4. Dress Bank for Women
Dress bank, an initiative conceived with the aim of providing wedding dresses to girls and clothes to the needy was started. All the well-wishers can participate in the initiative by donating wedding dresses to the dress bank. We were able to donate dress to an underprivileged and tribal people and other people in need as well. To co ordinate the activities of dress Bank, a four member committee has been costituted and it has been decided Tuesday as Dress Bank Day.

5. Women Enterpreneurship
In the last week of May 2022, we conducted a fiesta named lafesta. It was an exhibition conducted by women consisting of arts, craft, cloth, food, paintings. There were 56 stalls and the program helped the participants to improve their business. As part of the annual celebrations of the association, the association also conducted stalls for women entrepreneurs to exibit their products like paintings and handicrafts, and textiles bookstalls, boutique, food stalls etc.

6. Adopting families in deplorable condition
Koode adopted a number of families living in deplorable conditions and visited them once in a month and provided medical assistance and monthly food assistance. Repaired and rebuilt houses for people living in highly pathetic condition. It found remarkable improvement in health and wellness.

7. Women empowerment
A book was published in malayalam which was actually a dream come true of many women to showcase their literary works to society. It consists of 20 articles including stories, poems and life experience. Book was released by shri. Shoukath sahajotsu an eminent spiritual writer and speaker shedding lights to life of many around the globe. We sponsored an artificial limb to a 19 year old, doing her under graduation in a renowned college in Malappuram district she had congenital absence of her right leg. Legal Awareness Class was conducted by Legal Services Authority and it was informative on “Women and Laws”. A detailed class was also conducted on POCSO offences, and legal aid for it.

8. Koode Kitchen
A tiny step initiated by a group of women, in pursuit of self employment. Fresh vegetables are - washed, peeled,cut and then hygienically prepared mouth watering homely food delivered at the doorsteps by KOODE KITCHEN. The kitchen is slowly becoming the talk of the town.

9. Awareness programmes
Koode has conducted several awareness programmes to make women aware of their duties, rights and social responsibility.
  • Art festivals were conducted in colleges to help social change by creating movement in human mind.
  • A self-defence class was organized for women. Smt. K. Vatsala (Senior Civil Police Officer) led the class. Zumbathon was a unique event organized to make women aware of the need for exercise and the positive changes it brings to the body and mind.
  • A two-day workshop was organized with the aim of personality development interpersonal relationships, communication, and the mental structure of a person. The workshop was conducted by smt. P.K Saru (Transactional Analyst) on June 20, 21 1920
  • Dr. Sasidharan conducted a Basic Life Support training to learn life saving first aid lessons to be given in emergency situations.
  • A 4-minute long video was circulated on social media to protest against the increasing violence faced by women and children.
  • On February 14, 2021 Valentine's Day, with the aim of making the city clean and beautiful by loving nature, the city has been cleaned and bottle booths have been set up in various parts of the city. Along with this, a flash mob was also presented, giving the message of responsible waste management.
  • Aiming to eliminate plastic. It has been decided to bring awareness of waste collection and disposal in association with the municipal schemes and to sensitize the people to use the facilities under the municipality.
  • Conducted awareness classes at ward level in Perinthalmanna mulcipality and surrounding panchayats to go green, avoid disposable plastics and effective communications and promote women to use menstrual cups.
