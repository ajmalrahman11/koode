‘Koode’, a community of women enthusiasts, committed in serving in the field of philanthropic services to the victims of Domestic violence, Sexually Abused Children and Women, Disabled, Mentally Challenged Persons, Widows and Aged people. We provide financial help for hapless girls for education, palliative assistance to the helpless patients, adopting families living in deplorable conditions.

Members of ‘Koode’ is socially committed women, consisting of lawyers, doctors ,teachers, civil servants professionally qualified social workers and counsellors involved in supporting our fellow beings who are shattered and traumatized due to different social issues, and unstable economy.
Reaching across the barriers of casts and religions,politics and poverty,health and education,koode offers friendship and companionship,safe space for talents and creativity,skill development and support,educational and health support,legal and financial help,to provide hope encouragement and sanctuary to women and the most vulnernerable in the society.
Our vision is to build a society of women free from the trauma of abuse, crime, devastation, desolation, disability, fear, ill-health, ignorance, illeteracy, and isolation.

More light into who we are

To end crime against women and children, Koode, a registered charity came into existence on 9th February 2019 with a heart warming and powerful inaugural programme including dance, music and drama, telling the stories about women in crisis.

The programme was attended by women from all walks of life and men who supported the movement. Invited speaker Sarah Joseph (Renowned novelist and short story writer in Malayalam and winner of the prestigious national Kendra Sahitya Akademi Award and state Vayalar Award for the novel Aalahayude Penmakkal), Radhamma(Eeks out a living doing door to door ironing service), and Koode board members.

Some insight into our programmes

Day care centre for elderly persons

‘Koode' is Conducting Day care centre with Perintalmanna palliative care Society. Every Saturday from 9.00 am to 4 pm for the elderly people who are confined within the four walls of the house. It has been decided to prepare a day care, to listen, entertain and serve nutritious food for them.

Women Enterpreneurship

In the last week of May 2022, we conducted a fiesta named lafesta. It was an exhibition conducted by women consisting of arts, craft, cloth, food, paintings. There were a total of 56 stalls from the women entrepreneurs and the program helped the participants to improve their business.

Dress Bank for Women

Dress bank, an initiative conceived with the aim of providing wedding dresses to girls and clothes to the needy was started. All the well-wishers can participate in the initiative by donating wedding dresses to the dress bank. A four member committee has been costituted and it has been decided Tuesday as Dress Bank Day
